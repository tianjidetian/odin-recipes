Salmon with Garlic Lemon Butter Sauce

Description

Pan Seared Salmon with Garlic Lemon Butter Sauce is one of the tastiest, easiest recipes you’ll ever try! Tender salmon fillets are pan seared until perfectly golden brown then covered in a simple, vibrant and rich lemon butter sauce.

The lemon butter sauce for salmon is made up of 4 basic ingredients (garlic, butter, fresh lemon juice and chicken broth) and once you try it you’ll want to add it to everything.This pan fried salmon recipe is a restaurant-worthy dish that you can make in the comfort of your own home in 20 minutes!
